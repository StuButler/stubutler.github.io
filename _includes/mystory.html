Hi, I'm Stuart Butler, I'm currently the Course Director of Games Technology at Staffordshire University and an Authorized Unreal Instructor.

 

I've been working in games education since 2009 when I joined Staffordshire University as a Lecturer after releasing the second and third entries into the TT Superbikes series on Playstation 2 with Jester Interactive in 2008. 

 

I have published titles as a vehicle art, games design, animation and technical design/art roles as well as contributed to academic publications in Supply Chain Management and Virtual Reality with a mix of academics from across Europe.

 

More recently I have contributed to Epic Games' online course provision with courses based around the Stack Bot project introducing new users to building third person characters and collectable item games.

 

This website documents my previous projects as well as newer pieces I plan to post in the coming months.
